There is co-relationship between teacher and taught. I have rendered my service with commitment and devotion at various organizations, Member Science and Chairman, Pakistan Science Foundation, Islamabad in BPS-22, Director General Pakistan Scientific and Technological Information Centre (PASTIC), Director Research ORIC Sindh Agriculture University, Tandojam, Chairman Department of Form Structure and Dean Faculty of Agriculture Engineering, Sindh Agriculture University, Tandojam. I also supervised 110 students projects of undergraduate (Bachelor of Engineering), Postgraduate (Master of Engineering) and Ph.D. I also embarked number of research projects especially on Onion Post Harvest Technology. I have extensively visited for the purpose of Academic meetings and seminars to U.K, Iran, Azerbaijan, Thailand, Qatar, South Korea, Abu Dhabi, Saudi Arabia and China. I am fully confident and optimistic that the respected teachers, officers and employees of the University will contribute in right direction to uplift the soft image of this Institution and render their untiring services for the cause of education, community service and research.
